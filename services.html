Services

Focused, premium finishes for the surfaces you see — and touch — every day.

Veridian Paint Co. is intentionally specialized. Instead of trying to cover everything, we focus on interior walls, trim, cabinets, and high-wear surfaces where prep and detail matter most.

Each project starts with a walkthrough to understand how you use the space, what you want it to feel like, and how durable the finish needs to be. From there, we design a coating system — primer, topcoat, and sheen — matched to that reality.

Interior Essentials

Clean, durable finishes for daily living spaces.

Wall & Ceiling Painting

Full repaints or targeted refreshes for living rooms, bedrooms, hallways, and common areas. Includes repairs, caulking, and proper priming where needed.

  • Nail pops, minor cracks, and small repairs addressed before paint.
  • Walls and ceilings rolled and cut with sharp, consistent lines.
  • Sheens selected for how the room is actually used (washability, glare, etc.).

Accent Walls & Feature Areas

Designed focal points that anchor a room — from subtle tone shifts to bold, modern accent colors.

  • Color placement advice to balance the rest of the space.
  • Careful masking around built-ins, fireplaces, and media centers.
  • Compatible products chosen so the accent ages with the rest of the room.

Trim & Detail Work

Where the lines, edges, and transitions really matter.

Baseboards, Casings & Crown

Fine-finish enamels on baseboards, door casings, window trim, and crown moulding — tuned for crisp lines and durability.

  • Gap caulking and fastener holes filled before finish coats.
  • Sanding between coats where appropriate for a smoother profile.
  • Careful cutting where trim meets floors, walls, and ceilings.

Doors & Stair Components

Interior doors, handrails, newel posts, and balusters prepped and painted for both touch and wear.

  • De-glossing, scuff sanding, and cleaning to promote adhesion.
  • Fast-drying enamels selected for high-contact areas.
  • Hardware masked or removed based on project needs.

Cabinets & Specialty Surfaces

Upgrades for the highest-traffic, most-visible parts of your home.

Kitchen & Built-In Cabinet Refinishing

Multi-step refinishing systems that update the look of your cabinets without full replacement.

  • Degreasing, sanding, and repair of doors, drawer fronts, and frames.
  • Bonding primers suited to existing finishes (factory, stained, or previously painted).
  • Durable topcoats chosen for kitchens and high-use storage areas.

Built-Ins, Shelving & Niches

Bookshelves, media built-ins, mudroom cubbies, and other custom storage painted to feel intentional, not like an afterthought.

  • Repairs and caulking so seams read cleanly once finished.
  • Color and sheen guidance to coordinate with surrounding trim and walls.
  • Finish systems designed for frequent use and cleaning.

Repair & Maintenance

Keeping existing spaces looking sharp and cared-for.

Drywall Repair & Surface Prep

Small and medium-scale repairs blended into surrounding surfaces before repainting.

  • Cracks, nail pops, and minor dents repaired and sanded smooth.
  • Spot-priming and sealing stains before finish coats.
  • Texture blending where required for a consistent appearance.

Touch-Ups & Commercial Maintenance

Targeted repaints for offices, studios, and small commercial spaces to keep them presentable without a full shutdown.

  • Work scheduled to minimize disruption to staff or customers.
  • Matching or updating existing color schemes.
  • Maintenance plans available for repeat needs.

How pricing works

Every project is quoted individually based on condition, access, and scope. During the walkthrough we’ll:

  • Review which rooms, surfaces, and repairs are included.
  • Discuss coating options, color changes, and any schedule constraints.
  • Provide a written quote with clear line items so you know what’s covered.

If you’re comparing quotes, we’re happy to explain how our prep, materials, and process line up so you can make an informed decision — even if you don’t choose us.

Tell us what you’re working with — walls, trim, cabinets, or something else — and we’ll help you scope it.
